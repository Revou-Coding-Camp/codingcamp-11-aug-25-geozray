Our Profile

Geo Movement Indonesia (GEOMENT.ID) was founded in 1933 by Azri Ozray as a provider of measurement tools and measurement services located in Bandung. PT. Geo Movement Indonesia provides a focus on sales and rental services for measurement survey equipment. The areas served by us during the company's journey include providing sales and rental services for measurement and mapping survey equipment in the fields of Irrigation, Mining, Plantations and Forestry. In line with the development of the company, we also provide services in the field of survey services and calibration services for survey tools. With the support of professional and experienced human resources and complete and reliable supporting equipment, we hope to become a strategic partner for all clients in providing the solutions they need.

Our mission is to provide the best geospatial solutions and services to our clients, ensuring accuracy, reliability, and innovation in every project we undertake.

Our vision is to be a leading provider of geospatial solutions, recognized for our expertise, quality, and commitment to excellence in the industry.
